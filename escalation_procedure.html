First Line Support

Initial client questions and problems are handled by the first line of service. Among their duties are:

  • Collecting data and employing standard techniques for troubleshooting.
  • Solving simple issues with clear documentation.

Second-Level Support Escalation

The following situations result in escalation to second level support:

  • The problem is complicated or calls for specialized knowledge.
  • All of the first troubleshooting steps have been used.
  • The customer's problems are urgent or important.
  • First line support needs direction or is uncertain about the answer.

Third-Level Support Escalation

Third-level support escalation is required when:

  • The problem calls for highly skilled technical or architectural knowledge.
  • The problem cannot be resolved by second level support.
  • Critical or high priority concerns need to be addressed right away.
  • A possible security flaw or system vulnerability exists.
