Troubleshooting Questions

Initial Question

  • What problem are you encountering?
  • What led to the issue? (steps that brought about the problem)
  • Could you provide a description of the error message, if there is one?

Gathering Information

  • Which software or application is impacted? (e.g., Title, Dream)
  • Which operating system do your devices run on?
  • Are you utilizing any external plugins or extensions?
  • Have there been any recent changes made? (hardware, software, configuration)

Isolation

  • Are you able to replicate the problems? (uniformity - consistent and reliable issues)
  • Do the problems happen regularly or sporadically? (rate of occurrence)
  • Have you attempted to use a different account?

Configuration and Environment

  • Have you checked for loose connections or hardware issues? (physical inspection)
  • Is your device up to date? (latest version of OS or software)

Advanced Troubleshooting

  • Can you provide logs or screenshots?
  • Have you checked for firmware updates?
  • Have you tried restarting the device?
  • Are there any custom settings or configurations? (device settings)
